Jiachen Lu

Phd Student

I am a 2nd year PhD candidate in Biostatistics at UTHealth. I'm interested in both method development and data analysis. My statistical research involves Hidden Markov Models, Bayesian inference and Functional Data Analysis. I'm also an enthusiast of digital health and wearable device data, which involves identifying circadian rhythm from accelerometer data of physical activity and feature extraction from such data as digital biomarker. The main goal of my research is to manifest risk factors for health conditions and improve public health.
