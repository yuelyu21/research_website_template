Circadian Metabolic Health

This project aims to build rest-activity profiles from wearable device data and determine the associations with health outcomes. We develop novel statistical methodologies to quantify rest-active behaviors and construct rhythmic statistics to comprehensively characterize the circadian rhythm and its underlying relationships with health conditions, demographics, and socioeconomics.

Artificial Light at Night (ALAN)

This project aims to characterize average Artificial Light at Night (ALAN) levels and temporal trend in ALAN between 2012 and 2019 in all U.S. continental counties to identify areas with the highest and lowest ALAN exposures, and with the largest increase and decrease in ALAN during this period.
