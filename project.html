Find Our Research



Investigate the geospatial patt

ALAN measures were summarized for each of 3,108 counties in the continental US (excluding Alaska and Hawaii) and the District of Columbia. We consider mean county ALAN radiance (mean radiance) for each year between 2012 and 2019, calculated as the average ALAN within the county geographic boundary. This measure reflects the overall ALAN level, and higher values indicate higher exposure. We also consider the within-county variation of ALAN radiance (WCV), calculated as the variability of the ALAN radiance within a county geographic boundary.
